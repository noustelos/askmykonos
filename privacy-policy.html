Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/05/2026

AskMykonos.ai respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed when you use askmykonos.ai.

1. Data controller

The data controller is AskMykonos.ai / Noustelos Studio.

For any data protection request, you can contact:
info@askmykonos.ai

2. What data we collect

When using askmykonos.ai, the following data may be collected:

  • Information you voluntarily send by email, such as name, email address, company name, request details, partnership interest or message content.
  • Messages you type into the AI chat, only for the purpose of generating an AI response.
  • Basic technical connection data, such as IP address, user-agent, timestamp and request metadata, through hosting, security and infrastructure providers for security, abuse prevention and site reliability.
  • User preferences stored locally in your browser, such as language selection and cookie/banner preferences.
  • Browser microphone permission status and dictated text if you choose to use the microphone feature. Voice input is handled through your browser’s native speech recognition functionality where supported.
  • Basic interaction data necessary for operating the website and AI chat.

Do not submit sensitive personal data, payment details, passport details, health information or confidential information through the AI chat.

3. AI chat and automated responses

AskMykonos.ai provides an AI-powered travel assistant for visitors to Mykonos.

When you send a message to the chat, your message is sent from the website to a Cloudflare Worker and then processed through Google Gemini technology in order to generate a reply.

The AI assistant is designed to provide practical travel guidance, local suggestions and visitor information. AI-generated answers may be incomplete, inaccurate or outdated. You should verify current details such as schedules, prices, availability, weather, bookings, safety-sensitive information and transport options directly with the relevant provider or official source.

AskMykonos.ai is an independent visitor guide and is not an official tourism authority.

4. Purpose and legal basis of processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Responding to contact, partnership or business requests.
    Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(b), steps prior to entering into a contract or service communication.
  • Providing the AI chat functionality and generating responses to user questions.
    Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(b), providing the requested digital service, and/or Article 6(1)(f), legitimate interest in operating the service.
  • Security, abuse prevention, technical stability and fraud prevention.
    Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(f), legitimate interest.
  • Compliance with legal obligations where required.
    Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(c).
  • Remembering user interface preferences such as language choice.
    Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(f), legitimate interest, where only essential preference storage is used.

5. Cookies and local storage

AskMykonos.ai may use essential cookies or local storage for basic website functions, such as:

  • language preference
  • cookie/banner preference
  • interface preferences

AskMykonos.ai does not currently use advertising or profiling cookies.

If analytics, advertising pixels or third-party tracking tools are added in the future, this policy should be updated and, where required, consent should be requested before such tools are activated.

6. Microphone and dictation

The microphone button is optional.

If you use voice dictation, your browser may request microphone permission. Speech-to-text is handled through the browser’s native Web Speech API or equivalent browser functionality where supported.

AskMykonos.ai does not intentionally store raw audio recordings. The recognized text may be inserted into the chat input and sent to the AI assistant if you submit it.

If your browser or device does not support speech recognition, the microphone feature may be unavailable.

7. Recipients and processors

Personal data may be processed by technical providers necessary for operating the website and AI chat, including:

  • Cloudflare, for hosting, security, Worker infrastructure and request handling.
  • Google Gemini / Google AI services, for processing AI chat messages and generating responses.
  • Email service providers, when you contact AskMykonos.ai by email.
  • Website hosting, DNS, security or infrastructure providers where necessary.

Personal data is not sold.

8. International transfers

Some technical providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where this occurs, such transfers should rely on appropriate safeguards under applicable data protection law, such as adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms where required.

9. Partner recommendations and affiliate links

AskMykonos.ai may include partner recommendations, affiliate links or sponsored local suggestions.

These recommendations are intended to be relevant to the user’s request. Partner recommendations do not mean that AskMykonos.ai guarantees availability, quality, safety, price, timing or suitability of a provider.

Users should confirm all details directly with the relevant provider before booking or purchasing.

10. Data retention

Communication data is retained only for as long as necessary to respond to your request, manage cooperation, provide services or comply with legal obligations.

Technical logs are retained for a limited period according to the policies of the relevant hosting, security or infrastructure providers.

AI chat messages may be processed temporarily to generate responses and maintain service reliability. AskMykonos.ai should not be used to submit confidential or sensitive personal information.

11. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you may have the following rights, where applicable:

  • right of access
  • right to rectification
  • right to erasure
  • right to restriction of processing
  • right to object
  • right to data portability
  • right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

You can exercise your rights by contacting:
info@askmykonos.ai

12. Supervisory authority

If you believe that the processing of your personal data violates applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority:

www.dpa.gr

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The latest version should always be available at:
https://askmykonos.ai/privacy-policy.html
