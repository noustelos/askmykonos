# Beaches in Mykonos

Purpose:
Help visitors choose beaches based on mood, transport, family needs and wind.

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## Quick Guidance

- Ornos: practical, organized, easier for families and visitors staying near the southwest side.
- Platis Gialos: organized, good access, useful base for beach-hopping by water taxi when operating.
- Psarou: premium, lively, often associated with upscale beach clubs.
- Paraga: social, beach bars, sunset-friendly energy depending on the plan.
- Paradise and Super Paradise: party-oriented, better for nightlife and beach club mood.
- Agios Ioannis: calmer sunset-facing option, useful for couples and relaxed plans.
- Agios Sostis: quieter, less organized, better for visitors with transport and simple expectations.
- Fokos: remote and quiet, easier with a car or private transfer; check road and wind comfort.

## Wind-Safe Thinking

- Northern beaches can feel exposed when winds are strong.
- More sheltered, organized beaches such as Ornos or Platis Gialos may be easier for a simple beach day.
- If sea conditions are poor, suggest Mykonos Town / Chora, Ano Mera, food, museums or shopping instead of forcing a beach plan.

## How To Answer Beach Questions

- Ask where the visitor is staying if they ask "near me".
- Mention transport limits, parking and taxi scarcity when relevant.
- Mention wind and sea conditions without claiming live weather.
- Give 2-4 options, not a huge list.
- Match the recommendation to the visitor's mood: easy, family, scenic, beach clubs, quiet or premium.

## Good Answer Pattern

1. Best practical option
2. Why it fits
3. Alternative
4. Transport or wind note

## Do Not Invent

- exact beach club prices
- current sunbed prices
- live crowd levels
- current safety restrictions
- guaranteed water taxi operation
