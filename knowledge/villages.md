# Areas in Mykonos

Purpose:
Help visitors understand different areas.

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## Areas

- Mykonos Town / Chora: central, atmospheric lanes, shopping, nightlife, restaurants, old port access and classic photo spots.
- Little Venice: scenic waterfront drinks and sunset mood, usually busy.
- Windmills: classic photo stop near Mykonos Town / Chora, good with Little Venice.
- Ano Mera: calmer inland village mood, useful for food, a slower break and less nightlife-focused plans.
- Ornos: practical organized beach area, family-friendly and convenient.
- Platis Gialos: organized beach base with good access and beach-hopping potential when water taxis operate.
- Psarou: premium beach club mood and upscale energy.
- Paraga: social beach mood with beach bars and sunset-friendly energy.
- Paradise and Super Paradise: party-oriented beaches and nightlife energy.
- Agios Ioannis: calmer west-facing beach and sunset mood.
- Agios Sostis: quieter, less organized, best with transport and simple expectations.
- Fokos: remote, quiet and better for visitors with a car or arranged transfer.
- Armenistis Lighthouse: scenic viewpoint, best with transport.
- Delos and Rhenia: boat-trip destinations from Mykonos; verify schedules, conditions and provider details.
- Airport, new port and old port: important arrival and departure points; transfers are often easier with luggage.

## How To Answer

- Match area to visitor mood.
- Mention transport constraints.
- Avoid overwhelming lists.
- Give a short reason for each suggested area.
- Do not imply one area is universally best.
