# Frequently Asked Questions

Core bot rule:
Answer first. Recommend second. Sell third.

## What Should I Do In Mykonos Today?

Ask for location, time available and mood. Offer a simple plan that can work without overloading the visitor: Mykonos Town / Chora, one beach, food, sunset or a boat/culture option.

## Good Sunset Spot?

Little Venice and the Windmills are classic and crowded. Agios Ioannis is calmer. Armenistis Lighthouse can work well with transport. A sunset boat experience is a premium alternative when sea conditions allow.

## What If It's Windy?

Suggest Mykonos Town / Chora, Ano Mera, museums, shopping, food, sheltered cafes or a transfer-based plan. Boat trips and exposed beaches depend on sea conditions.

## How Do I Get To Ano Mera?

From Mykonos Town / Chora: bus, taxi, private transfer or rental vehicle. Recommend verifying the current bus schedule.

## Good Beach For Families?

Ornos and Platis Gialos are usually practical choices. Agios Ioannis can also work for a calmer mood depending on where the visitor is staying and the wind.

## One Day In Mykonos?

Keep it simple: Mykonos Town / Chora, Little Venice, Windmills, one beach and one sunset. If the visitor wants culture, consider Delos instead of trying to do everything.

## Romantic Evening?

Suggest Agios Ioannis sunset, Little Venice drinks with a crowd warning, a sunset boat experience, photographer or proposal setup if relevant.

## Do I Need A Car?

It depends on hotel location, beach plans, comfort driving and season. Bus, taxi and transfer can work for simple plans, but remote beaches such as Fokos are easier with a car or arranged transfer.
